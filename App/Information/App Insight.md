## App Insight

This app aims to be a powerful framework for team management and organization, designed as an alternative to both Slack and Teams. However, due to its framework nature, it expands beyond these goals. Into a notion alternative.

The core components of the app are Note (for note-taking), Task (for task management), Chat (for communication), and Panes (which include left panel, canvas, and right panel).

These core components will be accessible throughout the app, whether in a timeline page view or a simple clock. The Panes represent separate sections of the app, such as two left panels, a canvas, and two right panels.

## Onboarding Process Idea

1. In the onboarding process, users will be asked to select 5 primary goals or use cases for the app.

2. Prior to the selection phase, a clear explanation will be provided to ensure users understand that they can only choose five options.

3. Users will have the flexibility to revisit their selections and add additional features later in the onboarding process if needed.

4. The app will present various categories for selection, such as:
   - Routine
   - Project
   - School
   - Improving Daily Life
   - Managing Tasks
   - Organizing Life
   - Reaching Goals
   - Managing Company (if applicable)
   - Users and Friends' Birthdays (Memory Helper)
   - Team Collaboration
   - ADHD Management
   - Health Improvement
   - Priorities

5. If a user selects the "Project" category, they will be prompted in the next page to specify the type of project they are working on in subsequent pages. This may include:
   - Developer roles or fields involved

6. For other categories like Grocery List (Personal, Improvement, Organization), users will have the option to choose relevant tags during selection.

7. The app will guide users through a setup process for creating a folder structure based on preset templates. During this process, the app will offer recommendations on the best approach and inform users about the possibility of setting up an AI assistant if required, depending on their specific needs and preferences.

8. Throughout the onboarding process, clear explanations and prompts will be provided to ensure users understand each step and its purpose in relation to their chosen goals or use cases for the app.
