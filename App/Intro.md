## Graph of how things work Process

- **Onboarding**
	- Finds out why we'll be using the app
	- Helps us getting setup on app pages we'd like
	- Help structure our folders
	- Helps use acquainted in small ui font-size etc
	- Quickly explain how everything works
	
- **Our Frame Work App**
	- All pages are have expected schemas called blueprints.
	- LLM interacts with them well, and every thing simple to use
	- Pages blueprints are:
		- Data Blueprint
		- Plugin Blueprint
		- LLM Blueprint (MCP and Tools and Weather we support hot reload, where we work just like vite and accept llm changes, or notice them)
	- As data schema blueprint, many pages comes with plugin blueprint, where we can plugin external providers into our app.
- **Automation**
	- App will find your preference, helps you get up to date, and keep you updated on news
	- App will make your research for you, and will have a search engine page
	- Will help you stay organize, and help you schedule into empty slots.
